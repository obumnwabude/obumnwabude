About Me

Hey, I'm Obumuneme Nwabude and I am from Anambra State, in Nigeria. I'm a Konverge Media trainer for the Google Digital Skills for Africa program, sponsored by Google. I also spend time creating Progressive Web Apps, which are websites with focus of how users perceive them on mobile devices.

Being a Digital Skills Trainer

It was in mid 2019 that I was privileged to start training for Konverge Media on Digital Skills. It has been an exciting journey: full of learning, meeting people and getting connected. Here is a record of one of such trainings. Courtesy of being a trainer, I have learnt a great deal of digital marketing; both from experience and from varying online courses. One of them being from Google SkillShop.
